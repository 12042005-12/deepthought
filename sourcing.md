# Sourcing Methods


## Method 1: BioAsia Exhibitor Directory

Why it works:

Many biotech companies participate in BioAsia.

Limitation:

Some exhibitors are service providers.


---

## Method 2: DSIR Database

Why it works:

Companies recognized by DSIR usually invest in R&D.

Limitation:

Not every innovative company applies for DSIR recognition.


---

## Method 3: LinkedIn Jobs

Indicators:

SAP

ERP

Production

Manufacturing

Hiring

Why it works:

Hiring activity often signals growth.


---

## Method 4: India Pharma Expo

Contains specialty pharmaceutical manufacturers.

## Method 5: CII Member Directory
